Culture in Motion

NOWNESS is an award-winning, global video channel that screens the best in culture across art, design, fashion, beauty, music, food, and travel.

A movement for creative excellence in storytelling, NOWNESS celebrates the extraordinary in the everyday by programming, curating and creating with both established and emerging filmmakers including Bjork, Adwoa Aboah, Nadia Lee Cohen, Flying Lotus, Harley Weir, Luca Guadagnino, Solange and Virgil Abloh.

With films available to view in 10 different languages, NOWNESS connects a global audience to emotional and sensorial stories designed to provoke inspiration and debate.
