A future archive of identity

Dazed Beauty is a multi-platform channel that celebrates beauty conveyed through the lens of creativity, self-expression and identity.

In print, former Dazed Beauty cover stars include Kate Moss, Travis Scott and Kylie Jenner. Online, Dazed Beauty has grown to a global community with guest editors including Munroe Bergdorf, Mykki Blanco, Aquaria and Lil Miquela. And in the real world, Dazed Beauty launched the beauty counter of the future; a six-week experiential pop-up at Selfridges bringing the highly-influential, digital community of Dazed Beauty to life with some of the beauty and creative industry’s most exciting leading names including Sylvie Macmillan, Lucy Bridge and Charles Jeffrey, and Drag Race UK’s The Vivienne.
